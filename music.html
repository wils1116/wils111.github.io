Lauren Wilson

Music Background

Music has always been a strong passion of mine. At a young age I began playing the flute and piccolo in various concert bands as well as performing in musical theatre roles and choral programs. In high school, I auditioned for ILMEA and sang in the All State choir and All State Honors choir my junior and senior year, respectively. I was lucky enough to be ranked the top alto in District IX my senior year.

In 2012, I became a member of Ladies First A Cappella, Michigan State University's only all-female a cappella group. Here is where I fell in love with the contemporary a cappella genre and began to find my presence in the a cappella community. I am currently the music director of Ladies First, leading them to a showcase spot at the 2015 Kettering National A Cappella Festival, an opportunity to compete in the International Championship of Collegiate A Cappella, and hosted Michigan State's annual Accapalooza.

This coming January I have the great honor of teaching at my first a cappella festival at South Milwaukee High School! I will be teachning classes on vocal percussion, blending, and clinicing with high school groups. I am very excited to take this step in the a cappella community.
