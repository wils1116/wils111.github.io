About Me

Hello! My name is Lauren Wilson. I am currently a senior at Michigan State University, working on two majors: Marketing and Media and Information (Interactive and Social Media); as well as a minor in Information Technology. I'm originally from Aurora, Illinois, and graduated from West Aurora High School in 2012. Both of my paternal grandparents attended Michigan State, which led to me applying to MSU and began my journey to East Lansing.

One of my biggest passions in life is contemporary a cappella. Currently I am the music director for MSU's Ladies First A Cappella. I have been a member for since fall 2012 and hope to continue singing in my future. I love networking within the a cappella community through going to festivals and attending Camp A Cappella for the past few summers. I have just begun arranging for my group and hope to arrange professionally in the future. Check out more on the music page!

I hope to work in the entertainment industry after my college career. While I am still unsure of exactly where I want to end up in the professional world, I know that I enjoy performing and would love to be on the marketing side of anything in show business! Check out my resume for more on my professional life.

My other interests include Michigan State football and basketball, NBC sitcoms, vocal percussion, boating, traveling, and spending time with my friends and family!
